    Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

    Is there a free trial available?

    Yes, you can try us for free for 30 days. If you want, we’ll provide you with a free, personalized 30-minute onboarding call to get you up and running as soon as possible.

    Can I change my plan later?

    Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Simply go to your account settings and select the plan that best suits your needs.

    What is your cancellation policy?

    Our cancellation policy allows you to cancel your plan at any time. If you cancel, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle, and you won’t be charged for the next cycle.

    Can other info be added to an invoice?

    Yes, additional information, such as your company name, address, or tax identification number, can typically be added to an invoice. You can update your billing details in your account settings or contact support for assistance.
